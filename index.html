Our Journey

Shobha Videos is not just a business — it’s a journey built with hard work, trust, and years of service.

📀

The DVD Days

In the early days, our founder used to carry a DVD player to customer homes and show movies directly. That was the beginning of Shobha Videos.

💿

CD Rental Era

After some years, every house started buying DVD players. Customers began renting or buying CDs and DVDs. This continued for several years with strong customer support.

📺

Cable TV Revolution

Then cable TV arrived through wire connections. We expanded our service to provide stable cable TV to many homes in our area.

📡

Set-Top Box Upgrade

As technology improved, set-top boxes became common. We upgraded our systems and helped customers switch smoothly to the new method.

🌐

Internet Services

Today, we are growing again — now providing high-speed internet for homes and businesses. Same trust, new technology.

🤝

Trust Since Day One

From DVDs to internet, our journey is built on customer trust, honest service, and quick support. We continue to serve with the same dedication.
